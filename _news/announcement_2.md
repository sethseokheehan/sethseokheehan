Our paper **“Real-Time Black Ice Detection in Drone View Using YOLOX”** was published in the *Journal of Student Research* (vol. 13, no. 2). The work presents a YOLOX-based pipeline for drone-view black ice detection and mapping. DOI: [10.47611/jsrhs.v13i2.6831](https://doi.org/10.47611/jsrhs.v13i2.6831).
